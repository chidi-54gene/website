The 54gene Health Information Ecosystem™

54gene is solving the genomic data disparity gap with our robust health information ecosystem, which consists of the 54gene Biobank accompanied by highly curated clinical, phenotypic and genetic data. Our platform generates insights to solve healthcare’s biggest global challenges.

A hybrid platform built to solve global health challenges

To discover effective therapeutic targets for debilitating diseases with little to no cures, new biological insights derived from studying diverse genomes must be uncovered. We are building a one stop shop that enables us to connect patients with the most effective therapies and diagnostics.

Partners with a mutual interest in drug discovery are increasingly requiring data cohorts that cover diverse populations to undertake novel research, enhance disease understanding, and make new discoveries.

The 54gene Biobank: Enabling insight generation for a new wave of therapeutics

The 54gene Biobank, our state-of-the-art biorepository which stores samples and the accompanying clinical, phenotypic and genetic data in optimum conditions to enable insight generation for a new wave of therapeutics.

Our highly curated, fully harmonized data repository follows stringent quality control protocols designed to make data access seamless and enable our partners to derive actionable answers from our data sets.
